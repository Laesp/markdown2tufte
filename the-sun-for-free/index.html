Can I get a free copy of The Sun?

I read The Sun. Its a great publication, generally neutral with an open-minded acceptance to all sorts of writing. One of the most exciting parts is the Reader’s Write, which is best explained by The Sun:

Readers Write asks readers to address subjects on which they’re the only authorities. Writing style isn’t as important as thoughtfulness and sincerity. We publish only nonfiction in Readers Write.

One of the things about submitting to the Readers Write is that if they use the piece, The Sun will grant you a complimentary one-year subscription!

The Sun publishes only once a month, so we got to thinking, “How long would it take to get a entry into the Sun?”. In order to determine this, I’m going to enter a nonfiction writing piece every month and see how many months it takes to get it.
